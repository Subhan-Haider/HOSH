# Privacy Policy for HOSH

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

HOSH ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data within the HOSH browser extension.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
HOSH collects and processes the following data:
- **Website Content:** Our extension reads the text of quiz questions and options on pages within the `instructure.com` and `.edu` domains. This data is used solely to provide AI-powered answers.
- **API Keys:** The OpenRouter API key you provide is stored locally on your device via `chrome.storage.local`. It is only sent to OpenRouter.ai to facilitate AI requests.

## 2. Data Storage
- All user preferences and API keys are stored **locally** on your browser.
- We do not run any external backend servers that store your personal data or your quiz responses.

## 3. Data Transfer
- We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties.
- Question data is sent only to **OpenRouter.ai** for processing. Please refer to OpenRouter's privacy policy for how they handle transient data.

## 4. Permissions Justification
- `activeTab`: To read content from your active Canvas tab.
- `storage`: To save your API settings locally.
- `scripting`: To provide UI buttons on the page.
- `contextMenus`: To provide right-click solving features.

## 5. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
